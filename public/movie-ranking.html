The Best Movies According to Siddharth

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Athadu

A fugitive evades from crime by disguising as part of a traditional family who lost one of their own due to a childhood incident.

Pokiri

A cop goes undercover to kill as many criminals in his city as possible.

Magadheera

A warrior is reminded of his past life when he touches the girl he was in love with. The sage resumes in the current lifetime.
